Pivotal B2B Pipeline — QA Testing Script (Step-by-Step)
1) Core Pipeline & Lead Management

Kanban renders with default stages

Steps: Open Pipeline tab → Verify columns (New Lead, Engaged, Proposal Sent, Negotiation, Closed Won, Closed Lost).

Expected: All columns visible and correctly labeled.

Add/Edit custom stage

Steps: Admin → Settings → Pipeline → Add “Legal Review”.

Expected: Stage appears in board and all stage pickers; persists after refresh.

Drag & drop between stages

Steps: Move a card from New Lead to Engaged.

Expected: Stage updates instantly; activity feed logs the change; undo available (if designed).

Lead card essentials

Expected fields: Name, Company, Deal Value, Owner, Last Activity, Engagement Score badge.

Lead right panel

Steps: Click card.

Expected: Profile, Timeline, Notes, Tasks, Emails, Score Trend chart.

Manual lead creation

Steps: Sales → “Add Lead” → Save minimum fields.

Expected: Lead created in New Lead with Source = Manual; validation errors are clear.

Edit & audit logging

Steps: Change Deal Value and Owner.

Expected: Changes persist; immutable audit entry (who/what/when).

Global search & filters

Steps: Search by name/email/company; filter by Stage/Score/Source.

Expected: Instant results; filter chips reflect active state.

Ownership & permissions

Steps: Login as SalesA vs Marketing vs Admin.

Expected: Access limited per role; sensitive actions gated.

2) Lead Ingestion & AI Qualification

Asset Download auto-add

Steps: Submit public asset form.

Expected: Lead auto-created; Source = Asset Download; Stage = New Lead.

Proposal Form auto-add

Steps: Submit proposal request.

Expected: Lead created; Source = Proposal; Stage per rule (New Lead/Engaged).

AI screening of Contact Us

Seller intent → Not added to pipeline; tagged as Seller.

Service intent → Added to pipeline; Source = Contact Us.

Confidence threshold & overrides

Steps: Submit borderline inquiry.

Expected: Confidence score surfaced; admin override possible.

3) Newsletter & Bulk Campaigns (SMTP)

Campaign creation & segmentation

Steps: Segment Stage=Engaged & Score>50.

Expected: Audience count matches filter; preview resolves merge tags.

Personalization merge tags

Expected: {{FirstName}}, {{Company}}, etc. resolve; graceful fallbacks when null.

A/B testing (subject/body)

Expected: Split delivery; per-variant metrics.

Scheduling & timezone (Asia/Karachi)

Expected: Sends at scheduled local time; logs UTC and local consistently.

Compliance/unsubscribe

Expected: Footer contains company address + unsubscribe; clicking unsub updates status in real time.

Bounce handling

Expected: Bounces recorded; contact flagged; engagement score decremented.

Open/click tracking

Expected: Pixel/redirect events appear on lead timeline and campaign report.

4) M365 1:1 Email (Graph API) — Full Sync

OAuth connection

Expected: Least-privilege scopes; tokens encrypted; revoke disconnects cleanly.

Send 1:1 from lead panel

Expected: Message in Outlook “Sent Items”; logged on lead with thread ID.

Inbound reply sync

Expected: Webhook/poll creates timeline entry; preserves threading.

Open/click tracking for 1:1

Expected: Events recorded; engagement score updates; last activity refreshed.

Unknown inbound contact handling

Expected: Suggest create/associate lead when email from unknown appears.

5) Engagement Scoring (Dynamic)

Score rules

Open +10, Click +20, Multi-opens in 7 days +30, Extra asset +40, Proposal +50, Reply/Meeting +70, Bounce −10, Unsub −20.

Expected: Accurate increments/decrements; never negative (if rule set).

Threshold automations

Steps: Push score above 80.

Expected: Move to Engaged; notify owner; log automation.

Score visibility & trend

Expected: Badge on cards/list; historical chart per lead.

6) Automations & Reminders

Auto-advance on 2nd asset

Expected: Stage moves; activity logged.

No-reply 5-day rule

Expected: Follow-up task created for owner; due date set.

Proposal open notification

Expected: Owner notified within SLA (<1 minute).

7) Reports & Analytics

Summary cards

Expected: Total Leads, Value by Stage, Avg Score, Campaign Open/Click accurate.

Conversion funnel

Expected: Stage-to-stage ratios correct; drill-down lists contributing records.

Email performance

Expected: Opens/Clicks/Bounces/Unsubs match underlying message events.

Forecasted revenue

Expected: Sum(value × probability) equals widget; probabilities editable per stage.

Timezone integrity

Expected: Timestamps normalized to user locale; no off-by-one at day boundaries.

8) Security, Roles, Audit

Role-based access

Sales: manage leads, 1:1 emails; Marketing: campaigns; Admin: all.

Expected: Unauthorized actions blocked with informative messages.

Audit trail

Expected: Every critical change emits immutable audit entries.

Token security/rotation

Expected: Refresh works; rotation logged; revoked tokens deny access immediately.

9) API & Webhooks

Export pipeline data

Expected: CSV/JSON with correct schema; respects filters & permissions.

Import leads

Expected: Valid rows created; invalid rows rejected with granular error messages.

Webhook delivery & signing

Expected: HMAC-signed payloads; retries with exponential backoff.

10) Performance, Accessibility, Cross-Browser

Board performance with 2,000 leads

Expected: TTI < 3s; smooth scroll; virtualization enabled.

Campaign throughput & throttling

Expected: Respect provider limits; queue transparency; automatic retries.

Inbox sync latency

Expected: Events appear on timeline within agreed SLA.

Keyboard & screen reader support

Expected: Tab order logical; ARIA roles; skip links; focus visible.

Contrast & zoom

Expected: WCAG AA at 200% zoom; no layout break.

Cross-browser

Expected: Chrome/Edge/Firefox/Safari parity.

11) Data Quality & Compliance

PII masking/export controls

Expected: Sensitive fields masked in UI as configured; exports permission-gated.

GDPR/consent flags

Expected: EU contact without consent blocked from campaigns or requires explicit lawful basis.

12) End-to-End Happy Path (Single Scenario)

Journey: Download asset → Receives campaign → Opens & clicks → Sales sends 1:1 via Outlook → Lead replies → Requests proposal

Expected: Auto-add; tracking logged; score increases at each step; stage auto-advances; owner notified; reports reflect data accurately.

Deliverables & Acceptance

All tests above pass.

Known issues documented with severity/owner/ETA.

UAT sign-off from Sales and Marketing leads.

Rollback plan and monitoring dashboards prepared for go-live.
