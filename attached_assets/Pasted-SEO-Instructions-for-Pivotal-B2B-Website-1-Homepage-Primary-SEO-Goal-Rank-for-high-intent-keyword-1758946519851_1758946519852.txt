SEO Instructions for Pivotal B2B Website
1. Homepage

Primary SEO Goal: Rank for high-intent keywords like B2B lead generation, qualified leads for B2B, demand generation services, and account-based marketing.

Page Title (60–65 characters):
B2B Lead Generation & Demand Generation Services | Pivotal B2B

Meta Description (155–165 characters):
Generate qualified B2B leads with precision. Pivotal B2B helps you build predictable pipelines, reduce cost per lead, and scale revenue growth.

H1:
Fill Your Pipeline With Buyers Who Convert

H2 Suggestions:

Our Commitment to You

From Prospects to Pipeline to Profit

Stop Chasing Dead-End Leads. Start Building Revenue.

Why B2B Teams Choose Pivotal B2B

Target Keywords:

B2B lead generation

qualified B2B leads

demand generation services

ABM programs

B2B pipeline growth

On-Page Notes:

Include internal links to each service page.

Use FAQ schema at bottom for “What is B2B lead generation?” and “How does ABM drive revenue?”

Add alt tags to visuals (e.g., “B2B pipeline growth graph”).

2. Services Detail Pages (each service)

Primary SEO Goal: Rank for service-specific keywords like Account-Based Marketing Services or B2B Demand Generation Solutions.

Page Title Example:
Account-Based Marketing (ABM) Services | Pivotal B2B

Meta Description Example:
Engage entire buying committees with ABM programs. Pivotal B2B helps you influence decision-makers early and build predictable pipelines.

H1 (service-specific):
Account-Based Marketing Programs That Drive Revenue

H2 Suggestions (general template for all services):

Why [Service Name] Matters

Our Approach to [Service Name]

Benefits of [Service Name] with Pivotal B2B

Request a Proposal

Target Keywords (examples by service):

ABM: account-based marketing services, ABM lead generation

Lead Gen: B2B lead generation, qualified B2B leads, lead qualification

Demand Gen: demand generation services, intent-based marketing, educating buyers

Event Marketing: event audience acquisition, event lead generation

Lead Validation: lead validation services, clean lead data, B2B data enrichment

Nurturing: lead nurturing campaigns, B2B buyer engagement, nurture programs

On-Page Notes:

Add a structured CTA block with schema markup for “Request a Proposal.”

Insert case study snippet or testimonial per service.

Include internal links back to homepage + related services.

5. Sitewide SEO Enhancements

Technical SEO:

SSL, fast load speed (<3s), mobile optimization.

Sitemap.xml + robots.txt submitted.

Schema: Organization, Service, Review, FAQ.

Content SEO:

Each page ~1,200–1,500 words (Services can be 800–1,000).

Keyword density: 1–1.5% with natural language.

Include FAQs with semantic keywords.

Internal Linking:

Homepage → Services pages → Case Studies.

Each Service → Related Services + B2B Audience.

Footer: repeat nav with keyword-rich anchor text.

Blog (Content Hub):

Post 2–4 times/month around keywords: “ABM best practices,” “reduce cost per lead B2B,” “lead nurturing strategies.”

Link blog posts to service pages.
